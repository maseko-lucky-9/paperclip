# Rate Card — Prudentia Digital

**Currency**: South African Rand (ZAR), excluding VAT
**Effective**: 2026-01-01 | Review: 2027-01-01

---

## Standard Rates

### Hourly

| Tier | Rate (ex VAT) | Applicable Scenario |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | R850/hr | SME clients, straightforward advisory, relationship engagements |
| Commercial | R1,000/hr | Architecture review, solution design, technical due diligence |
| Premium | R1,200/hr | Crisis response, niche specialist work, last-minute availability |

**Minimum billing unit**: 2 hours.
**Travel time**: 50% of applicable rate for travel > 45 min one-way.
**After-hours** (18:00–08:00 weekdays): +25% surcharge on applicable rate.
**Weekends / Public holidays**: +50% surcharge on applicable rate.

---

### Day Rate

| Tier | Rate (ex VAT) | Applicable Scenario |

## VAT

- Prudentia Digital is a registered VAT vendor.
- All rates above are **exclusive of VAT** (15%).
- Invoices will reflect: subtotal (ex VAT) + VAT amount + total (inc VAT).
- Government clients: confirm VAT treatment with client's SCM office (some SOEs have exemptions or specific handling requirements).

## Rate Review Schedule

- Rates reviewed annually in January.
- CPI + 5% applied as a baseline increase to retainer clients on renewal.

- New rates communicated to retainer clients with 30 days' notice prior to the renewal invoice.
