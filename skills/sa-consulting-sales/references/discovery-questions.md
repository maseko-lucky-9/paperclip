# Discovery Question Bank

Organised by topic for SA consulting sales conversations.

## Situation / Context

- "Tell me about your current technology setup — what are you running today?"
- "How big is your technical team? Who manages infrastructure?"
- "What's your current hosting situation — on-prem, cloud, hybrid?"
- "Are you using any CI/CD or automated deployment?"
- "How do you handle monitoring and alerting today?"

## Problem / Pain

- "What's the biggest technology headache you're dealing with right now?"
- "If you could fix one thing about your current systems, what would it be?"
- "How much time does your team spend on manual processes that could be automated?"
- "Have you had any downtime or security incidents in the past year?"
- "What happens when load shedding hits your infrastructure?"
- "Are you confident your systems are POPIA-compliant?"

## Impact / Cost

- "What is this problem costing you? In time? Revenue? Customer experience?"
- "How does this affect your team's ability to deliver on other priorities?"
- "What's the risk of not addressing this in the next 6 months?"
- "Have you lost any clients or opportunities because of this limitation?"

## Budget / Investment

- "Have you allocated budget for this, or are we exploring what's possible?"
- "What range are you thinking about for this kind of project?"
- "How does your budgeting process work — annual cycle, project-based, or ad-hoc?"
- "Is there flexibility in timing to align with your next budget cycle?"

## Authority / Decision Process

- "Who else would be involved in making this decision?"
- "Does this need to go through a formal procurement process?"
- "Is there a preferred vendor list I should be aware of?"
- "What does the approval process look like for a project like this?"

## Timeline / Urgency

- "When do you need this done by? Is there a hard deadline?"
- "What's driving the timeline — a business event, compliance deadline, or something else?"
- "If we started next week, when would you need to see first results?"
- "Is there a tender deadline or financial year-end we need to work around?"

## Competition / Alternatives

- "Have you looked at other options for solving this?"
- "Have you worked with external consultants before? How was that experience?"
- "Are you considering building this in-house instead?"
- "What would make this a clear 'yes' for you?"

## Closing / Next Steps

- "Based on what you've told me, I think we can help. Would a detailed proposal be useful?"
- "Shall I put together a scoped approach with timeline and pricing?"
- "What would you need to see to feel confident moving forward?"
- "Is there anyone else I should include in the next conversation?"
