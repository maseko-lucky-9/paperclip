# CSD Registration & Maintenance Checklist

The Central Supplier Database (CSD) is mandatory for doing business with South African government entities. All government procurement officers verify suppliers on csd.gov.za before awarding contracts.

## Initial Registration Requirements

| Document / Information | Details |
|----------------------|---------|
| CIPC registration certificate | Company registration proof |
| SARS tax clearance (TCC) | Must show "Good Standing" |
| B-BBEE certificate or EME affidavit | Current, not expired |
| Banking details | Business bank account, verified with bank stamp |
| Physical address | Registered business address |
| Contact details | Email, phone, authorised representative |
| Director/member details | Full names, ID numbers |
| Company resolution | Authorising CSD registration |

## Registration Steps

1. Navigate to csd.gov.za and create an account
2. Complete the supplier registration form
3. Upload all required documents
4. Submit for verification
5. Wait for verification (typically 5-10 working days)
6. Once verified, you receive a CSD number (MAAA prefix)

## Maintenance Tasks

| Task | Frequency | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Tax clearance renewal | Annual (or as it expires) | CSD verifies tax status automatically with SARS |
| B-BBEE certificate renewal | Annual | Upload new affidavit before expiry |
| Banking details update | As needed | Requires re-verification |
| CSD report generation | Before each tender | Print dated within 7 days of submission |
| Company details update | As changes occur | Director changes, address changes |

## Common CSD Issues

| Issue | Resolution |
|-------|-----------|
| "Tax non-compliant" flag | Check SARS eFiling for outstanding returns or debt |
| Registration pending >10 days | Call CSD helpline: 012 406 9222 |
| Banking verification failed | Submit new bank confirmation letter (stamped by bank) |
| B-BBEE expired | Upload new affidavit; old one invalidates your CSD report |
| Duplicate registration | Contact CSD to deactivate the duplicate |

## CSD Report for Tender Submissions

When submitting a tender response:
1. Log into csd.gov.za
2. Generate a "Supplier Registration Report"
3. Print the report (it's dated and timestamped)
4. The report must be dated within 7 days of tender closing date
5. Include in the "Returnable Documents" section of your submission

The CSD report serves as proof of: company registration, tax compliance, B-BBEE status, and banking verification. Some departments accept only the CSD report (no separate documents needed).
