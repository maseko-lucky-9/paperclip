# Email Templates — Prudentia Digital

Full-length templates for standard business communications. See SKILL.md for guidance on when to use each template and how to adapt tone to audience.

**Sign-off convention**: All client emails end with "Kind regards" unless otherwise noted. Government official correspondence uses "Yours faithfully" for first contact.

---

## 1. Project Kickoff Confirmation

Sent within 24 hours of the kickoff meeting. This email is the definitive record of what was agreed — it drives the entire engagement.

```
Subject: [Project Name] — Kickoff Summary & Next Steps

Dear [Name] / Hi [Name],

Thank you for a productive kickoff session today. I've summarised the key agreements below
to ensure we're fully aligned before work begins.

SCOPE CONFIRMED
[2-3 sentences describing the agreed scope in plain language. Reference the SOW.]

AGREED MILESTONES
| Milestone | Target Date |
|-----------|------------|
| [Milestone 1] | [Date] |
| [Milestone 2] | [Date] |
| [Final delivery] | [Date] |

COMMUNICATION & REPORTING
- Weekly status updates: every [day] by [time]
- Primary contact on your side: [Name, role]
- Primary contact on our side: Thulani Maseko, thulani@prudentiadigital.co.za, [mobile]
- Escalation: Phone call first, then formal written notice

FIRST SPRINT — [Sprint 1 Goal]
Start: [Date] | End: [Date]

Sprint 1 priorities:
1. [Item]
2. [Item]
3. [Item]

OUTSTANDING ACTIONS
| Action | Owner | Due |
|--------|-------|-----|
| [Access required from client] | [Client contact] | [Date] |
| [Document to be shared] | [Client contact] | [Date] |
| Sprint 1 backlog finalised | Thulani | [Date] |

Please confirm if anything above is not as you understood it. Otherwise, I'll proceed
on this basis and you'll receive your first status update on [date].

Kind regards,
Thulani Maseko
Prudentia Digital
[mobile] | thulani@prudentiadigital.co.za
```

## 3. Delay Notification

Sent proactively — minimum 5 business days before a missed deadline wherever possible. Never send this after the client has already noticed. Use the Situation / Impact / Cause / Recovery / Commitment framework.

```
Subject: [Project Name] — [Milestone/Deliverable] — Timeline Update

Hi [Name],

I want to flag a timeline change proactively so we can manage it together.

SITUATION
[Deliverable/Milestone name] is tracking behind schedule. The current projected completion
date is [new date], [X days/weeks] later than the contracted target of [original date].

IMPACT
- [Downstream milestone name] will shift from [original date] to approximately [revised date]
- [Any billing milestone affected] — I'll confirm the revised invoice date separately
- [Any client-side dependency affected — e.g. "your UAT window will need to shift"]

CAUSE
[One factual sentence. Examples:
- "Integration with [third-party API] required additional rework due to undocumented breaking
  changes in their authentication layer."
- "Load shedding Stage 4 disrupted three planned delivery days during the week of [date]."
- "The database migration revealed data quality issues in the source system that required
  cleaning before we could proceed."]

RECOVERY PLAN
To recover, I am:
1. [Specific action — e.g. "Dedicating [X] additional hours this week to [task]"]
2. [Specific action — e.g. "Bringing in [contractor/specialist] to accelerate [component]"]
3. [Specific action — e.g. "Deferring [lower-priority item] to the next sprint"]

New target date: [Date] — I'm confident in this revised date.

COMMITMENT
[One sentence on what Thulani is personally doing differently to prevent recurrence, or
what he will do immediately to recover.]

I apologise for the disruption to your planning. I'll keep you closely updated over the
next [timeframe]. If you'd like to discuss this on a call, I'm available [offer 2 slots].

Kind regards,
Thulani Maseko
Prudentia Digital
[mobile] | thulani@prudentiadigital.co.za
```

## 4. Scope Change Request

Sent when the client requests work outside the agreed SOW. Work does not begin until written approval is received.

```
Subject: [Project Name] — Scope Change Request: [Brief Description]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for raising [the request — brief description]. This is a useful addition to
the project and I want to make sure we handle it properly.

SCOPE ASSESSMENT
The requested work — [1-2 sentences describing the request] — falls outside the current
Statement of Work ([SOW reference/date]), which covers [brief reminder of what's in scope].

ESTIMATE FOR ADDITIONAL WORK
| Item | Estimated Hours | Rate | Estimated Cost |
|------|----------------|------|----------------|
| [Task 1] | [X hrs] | R[rate]/hr | R[amount] |
| [Task 2] | [X hrs] | R[rate]/hr | R[amount] |
| **Total** | **[X hrs]** | | **R[total]** |

[If fixed price alternative is available: "Alternatively, I can deliver this as a fixed-price
addition at R[amount]."]

TIMELINE IMPACT
[Option A: "This can be delivered in parallel with the existing scope without affecting
current milestones."]
[Option B: "[Existing milestone name] will shift by approximately [X days] to accommodate
this work. If you'd like to avoid any delay, we can defer [lower-priority item] instead."]

TO PROCEED
Please reply to this email confirming your approval of the scope addition and associated
cost. I'll raise a Change Order for your signature before starting work.

If you'd like to discuss the options, I'm happy to set up a brief call.

Kind regards,
Thulani Maseko
Prudentia Digital
[mobile] | thulani@prudentiadigital.co.za
```

## 7. Payment Reminder — Stage 3 (Formal Overdue Notice)

Sent 14+ days after the due date. Firm, references the contract. Sent to the financial contact and copied to the primary engagement contact.

```
Subject: FORMAL NOTICE: Overdue Payment — Invoice [#INV-XXXX] — [Project Name]

Dear [Name],

This is a formal notice regarding Invoice [#INV-XXXX] for R[amount] (incl. VAT), issued
on [invoice date] and due on [due date]. The invoice remains unpaid [X] days after the
agreed payment date.

In terms of our Master Services Agreement dated [MSA date], payment terms are net [30/14]
days from invoice date. The MSA further provides for [interest at prime + X% / a late
payment fee of X%] on amounts outstanding beyond [X] days.

To avoid the accrual of interest charges, I request payment of R[amount] by
[specific deadline — 7 days from this notice].

Please confirm receipt of this notice and provide an expected payment date by reply.
If there is a dispute about this invoice, please notify me in writing by [same deadline]
so we can resolve it in accordance with the dispute resolution process in the MSA.

Yours faithfully,
Thulani Maseko
Prudentia Digital
[mobile] | thulani@prudentiadigital.co.za

[Attach: Invoice, MSA excerpt with payment terms clause]
```

## 9. Difficult Client Communication — Engagement Termination (Initiated by Prudentia)

Used only in serious situations: sustained non-payment, fundamental scope dispute, or ethical conflict. Reference the MSA termination clause.

```
Subject: [Project Name] — Formal Notice of Engagement Termination

Dear [Name],

In accordance with Clause [X.X] of our Master Services Agreement dated [date], I am
providing formal written notice of Prudentia Digital's intention to terminate the
[Project Name] engagement, effective [termination date — per notice period in MSA].

REASON
[Factual, single-paragraph statement. Example:
"Despite three formal payment requests dated [date], [date], and [date], invoices
[#INV-XXXX] and [#INV-XXXX] totalling R[amount] remain unpaid. This constitutes a
material breach of the payment terms in Clause [X] of the MSA."]

WORK STATUS AT TERMINATION
Work completed to date will be retained in its current state. I will not undertake
further billable work after [date].

HANDOFF OBLIGATIONS
By [date], I will:
- Transfer all code, documentation, and credentials to [agreed location/contact]
- Revoke Prudentia's access to all client systems
- Provide a written summary of work completed and work outstanding

OUTSTANDING OBLIGATIONS
The following amounts remain due and payable:
- Invoice [#INV-XXXX]: R[amount], due [date]
- Invoice [#INV-XXXX]: R[amount], due [date]
- **Total outstanding: R[amount]**

These amounts remain due notwithstanding termination, as the underlying work has been
delivered. I reserve all rights to pursue recovery of outstanding amounts.

If you believe this notice has been issued in error or wish to discuss resolution before
[termination date], please contact me by phone on [mobile] as soon as possible.

Yours faithfully,
Thulani Maseko
Prudentia Digital
[mobile] | thulani@prudentiadigital.co.za

[Send via email with read receipt AND registered post for material breaches]
```
