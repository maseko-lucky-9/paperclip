# SARS Tax Compliance Calendar

Annual compliance deadlines for a sole proprietorship (Prudentia Digital).

## Monthly / Bi-Monthly

| Task | Frequency | Deadline | Notes |
|------|-----------|----------|-------|
| VAT return (VAT201) | Bi-monthly | 25th of month following period end | Only if VAT-registered |
| Employee tax (EMP201) | Monthly | 7th of following month | Only if employees are hired |
| Revenue reconciliation | Monthly | Last business day | Internal — reconcile invoices vs bank |
| Tax provision transfer | Monthly | Last business day | Transfer 30% of net profit to savings |

## Quarterly

| Task | Deadline | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| VAT turnover check | End of each quarter | Monitor 12-month rolling turnover vs R1M threshold |
| Cash flow forecast update | End of each quarter | Update 13-week rolling forecast |

## Annual — Tax Year (1 March to 28/29 February)

| Task | Deadline | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **First provisional tax (IRP6)** | 31 August | Based on estimated taxable income for the year |
| **Second provisional tax (IRP6)** | 28/29 February | Adjusted estimate, closer to actual |
| **Third provisional tax (optional)** | 30 September | Voluntary top-up to avoid interest on underpayment |
| **Annual income tax return (ITR12)** | Filing season (Jul–Nov) | SARS announces exact dates annually; typically opens July |
| **Tax clearance renewal** | Annual / as needed | Apply via SARS eFiling; required for government tenders |

## Annual — CIPC

| Task | Deadline | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Annual return (CoR30.1) | Anniversary of registration | R125 filing fee; late filing = non-compliance status |
| Update registered details | As needed | Director/member changes, address updates |

## Annual — B-BBEE

| Task | Deadline | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| EME sworn affidavit | Annually (no fixed date) | Free at Commissioner of Oaths; renew before expiry |
| Turnover threshold check | February | Confirm still below R10M EME threshold |

## Key SARS eFiling Links

- eFiling portal: efiling.sars.gov.za
- Tax clearance: Request via eFiling > Tax Compliance Status
- Provisional tax: Submit IRP6 via eFiling > Returns > Provisional Tax
- VAT registration: Apply via eFiling > Registered Taxes

## Penalties to Avoid

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Late provisional tax payment | 10% of tax due + interest |
| Underestimation (>20% below actual) | 20% of the difference |
| Late annual return filing | R250/month (admin penalty) up to R16,000/month based on income |
| Late VAT return | 10% penalty + daily interest |
| CIPC annual return late | Company status changes to "Non-compliant" |
