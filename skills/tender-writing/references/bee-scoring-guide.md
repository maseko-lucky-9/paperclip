# B-BBEE Scoring Guide for Government Tenders

## Preference Point Systems

South Africa uses two preference point systems under the PPPFA:

### 80/20 System (contracts up to R50 million)

| Component | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Price | 80 |
| B-BBEE status | 20 |
| **Total** | **100** |

### 90/10 System (contracts above R50 million)

| Component | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Price | 90 |
| B-BBEE status | 10 |
| **Total** | **100** |

## Prudentia Digital Position

- **Classification**: EME (Exempted Micro Enterprise)
- **Ownership**: 100% Black-owned
- **Automatic Level**: Level 1
- **B-BBEE Points (80/20)**: 20/20 (maximum)
- **B-BBEE Points (90/10)**: 10/10 (maximum)
- **Proof required**: Sworn EME affidavit at Commissioner of Oaths

## Price Score Formula

```
Ps = X × (1 - (Pt - Pmin) / Pmin)

Where:
  Ps = points scored for price
  X = price weight (80 or 90)
  Pt = your tender price
  Pmin = lowest acceptable tender price
```

### Example (80/20 system)

If lowest price is R500,000 and your price is R550,000:
```
Ps = 80 × (1 - (550,000 - 500,000) / 500,000)
Ps = 80 × (1 - 0.10)
Ps = 80 × 0.90
Ps = 72
```

Your total with Level 1 B-BBEE: 72 + 20 = **92 points**
Cheapest bidder (Level 3 B-BBEE): 80 + 14 = **94 points**

This means: a 10% price premium requires your competitor to be below Level 2 for you to win on B-BBEE alone.

## Subcontracting Requirements

The PPPFA and associated regulations require:
- Minimum **30%** of contract value must be sub-contracted to EME/QSE suppliers
- Some departments set higher thresholds (40-50%)
- Sub-contractors must be CSD-registered
- Sub-contracting plan must be submitted with the tender
